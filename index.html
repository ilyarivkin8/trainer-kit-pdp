Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before adding to cart.

BlazePod is a holistic reaction training platform that guides your training from start to finish. Use your Pods for activation, strength & conditioning, skills training, and rehabilitation — light-based visual cues that challenge you, your athletes, and your clients.

Browse the BlazePod app and choose pre-made activities, or create, save and share your own. Measure performance down to milliseconds, analyze results with instant data, and act on it.

By buying BlazePod, you select from a range of light Pods kits and bundles that serve as your reaction training platform. To use them, download the free BlazePod app — you'll receive our BlazePod Go plan immediately. To unlock the full coach toolkit, upgrade to BlazePod Pro anytime. You can also add accessories (PodBases, adapters, cones) to tailor training to your discipline.

BlazePod Go is free for casual users — 30+ pre-defined activities and the ability to build your own based on our most popular light logic.

BlazePod Pro is the premium membership for performance coaches, fitness trainers, and therapists. It adds custom activities, personalized client exercises, Athlete Performance Tests, motivational leaderboards, and in-depth analytics so you can see exactly where each athlete needs to focus.

Unbox your Pods, charge them on the base, download the BlazePod app, pair the Pods over Bluetooth, and pick a drill — you're running your first session within 5 minutes.
